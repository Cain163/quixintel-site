About Quix Intel

Your Strategic Partner in Global Security Intelligence

Quix Intel is an AI-powered geopolitical threat intelligence platform that provides real-time security assessments and predictive analysis across four major global regions: the Middle East, Latin America, Asia, and Africa.

Using advanced AI and natural language processing, our platform continuously monitors and scores threats (0-100 scale) by aggregating intelligence from verified news sources, social media channels, government communications, and specialized databases. Each event is automatically analyzed, scored for severity, and delivered through an intuitive mobile-first interface.

Designed for security analysts, corporate risk managers, journalists, government agencies, and travelers, Quix Intel makes professional-grade geopolitical intelligence accessible to anyone who needs situational awareness in volatile regions.

Important: All threat scores, summaries, and predictions are AI-generated and provided for informational purposes only. This platform should not be used as a substitute for professional security advice or official government travel advisories. Users must verify information with official sources before making security decisions.

Platform Features

Comprehensive Intelligence Capabilities

Real-Time Threat Scoring

Automated 0-100 threat scoring system powered by AI. Multi-factor analysis including event severity, geographic proximity, historical patterns, and real-time intelligence with predictive modeling.

AI Situation Reports

Automated intelligence summaries across multiple timeframes (24h, 48h, 7d). Structured reports with executive assessments, regional developments, key trends, and security outlooks.

Interactive Threat Maps

Full interactive maps with color-coded threat circles scaled by population. Tap any country for detailed threat scores, event counts, and situation assessments with boundary overlays.

AI-Powered Search

Natural language query engine for intelligence. Ask questions like "What happened in Gaza today?" and receive AI-analyzed answers drawn from real-time event data and verified sources.

Market Data & Economic Intel

Live tracking of BTC, ETH, and Brent Crude prices with 24h change indicators. Correlate financial market movements with regional security events for economic intelligence.

Multi-Source Intelligence

Three-tiered event system aggregating News, Chatter (social media/OSINT), and Wire (government communications). Each event scored for severity with translation support.

Configurable Alerts

SMS and email notifications for critical threat level changes. Customizable alert preferences with per-user threshold settings and pinnable notification management.

Mobile-First Platform

Native iOS and Android apps built with React Native. Dark theme optimized, pull-to-refresh dashboard, regional clocks, and executive assessment ticker with shareable reports.
